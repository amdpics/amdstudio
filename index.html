The Studio

10 Years.
One
Vision.

AMD STUDIO is an AI-powered creative agency based in Orange County, CA, built on a decade of hands-on experience in media production, content strategy, and brand storytelling.

Founded by a creator who has spent 10 years in media and content production — and the last 3 years at the forefront of AI-generated content — AMD STUDIO exists at the intersection of technical precision and visual artistry.

What started as a deep passion for automotive photography evolved into a full-service creative operation. As AI tools began reshaping the production landscape, AMD STUDIO was already ahead of the curve — actively integrating the latest models, software, and workflows to produce content that was faster, more scalable, and more visually powerful than traditional methods alone could achieve.

Today, AMD STUDIO serves automotive brands, apparel companies, and growth-focused businesses that refuse to settle for average content. Every project is approached with the same standard: world-class execution, delivered on time, built to perform.
